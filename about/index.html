About


Jerome F. Villegas is a local Seattle technical writer. He’s got an education background and open source community interest. Find him starting his day walking Howland, a talktative Husky mix, before kicking off work, and then ending his day with a journal entry summarizing as much as he can remember of the happenings.

He’s a professional technical writer and documentation strategist, emphathizing with users and experts to bridge the communication gap between the two. Jerome’s passion as a technical communicator is making sure that the content he creates helps people get things done. He also enjoys terrible puns, dad jokes, and general stressful humor.
