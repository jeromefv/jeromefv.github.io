Key Points from the UX Writer Conference “Advice for New Writers” Session

Jun. 30, 2020


Advice for New Writers Session

Jeremiah Telzrow and I collaborated on an article highlighting quality advice from UX/tech writers and content strategists at the UX Writer Conference held virtually on June 9th and 10th. We focused on collecting and referencing the suggestions that people had and produced visuals to make finding that content easier.

Getting started in the technical writing field is no easy task. We felt compelled to share the resources we’ve learned with others who feel the same. See the article at Jeremiah’s website!
