Aeropress Inverted Instructional Aid

Mar. 12, 2020

I developed this quick-reference card as a tool to aid people using the AeroPress. For those looking for a great and simple brewing method, the AeroPress opens up many possibilities of amazing coffee with effort comparable to a French press.

Using this document, cater your preferences of water, coffee, and immersion time to your own taste. Attach this to an AeroPress as a gift knowing the end result is a wonderful cup of coffee.
