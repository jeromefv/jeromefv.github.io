Guillain-Barré Syndrome Website

Jul. 30, 2020

Here, I built and launched a website about the medical disorder known as Guillain-Barré Syndrome (GBS).

I introduce and provide information about GBS in simple and straightforward terminology. Most content about this disorder uses highly specialized vocabulary catered to medical professionals. These obstacles make it challenging for patients and loved ones to understand this challenging and complex issue.

My aim as a technical writer is to increase accessibility for this subject and related topics. I use general sentences and language appropriate for all audiences. I also produced simple and easy to understand supporting visuals. Each image has captions, labels, and alt text for users with various needs. Throughout the website, I detail specifics of GBS with approachability and concision.
